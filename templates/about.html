About Us - Admission Insight

Project Overview

Admission Insight is a predictive analytics project that leverages machine learning to estimate the likelihood of admission into graduate programs. The model uses key parameters such as GRE Score, TOEFL Score, University Rating, Statement of Purpose (SOP), Letter of Recommendation (LOR), CGPA, and Research experience to generate predictions.

Problem Statement

Many students face uncertainty while applying to graduate schools. The challenge is to provide data-driven insights to help applicants determine their chances of admission. By using historical admission data, we aim to build a model that provides accurate predictions and assists students in making informed decisions.
